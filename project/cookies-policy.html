Cookies Policy

Upacrav uses cookies to enhance your experience while browsing our website and provide services. This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are, how they are being used, and your choices regarding them.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that your device stores on visiting a particular website. The cookies help a site recognize your preferences, remember your login details, and improve overall functionality for further use.

How We Use Cookies

Cookies are used for:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the website to function properly, such as enabling secure logins and cart management.
  • Analytical Cookies: To help us track our performance on the website, understand users' behavior, and improve services to you.
  • Functional Cookies: To help remember your preferences in order to add more personalization to your experience.
  • Marketing Cookies: They are used to provide relevant advertisements and measure the efficacy of any marketing campaigns.

How To Manage Cookies

You can switch off or control the usage of cookies via your browser settings. Bear in mind that some cookies, when blocked, would affect your experience on our site.

Third-party Cookies

We may use trusted third-party services that place cookies to analyze website traffic or deliver targeted ads. These third parties will have their own cookie policy.

In case of further inquiries regarding our Cookies Policy, please write to us at info@upacrav.com.
