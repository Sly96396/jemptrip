Return and Refund Policy

At Upacrav, we strive to ensure that each customer is completely satisfied with their Bitcoin mining machine purchase. If you are not very pleased with your order, you can avail a smooth return and refund process that is in line with the set norms here.

Returns Qualification

The product is accepted for return provided it is in original condition, unused and is returned with all accessories and packing in an intact manner. A request for return must be raised within 7 days of delivery and strict scrutiny of the returned product will be conducted.

Refund Procedure

A refund, therefore, shall be placed unto your bank account once the company receives and examines the returned product. Refunds might therefore take up to 14 business days to reflect into your bank account owing to the policies of banks and payment providers. Processing fees, if any, may be deducted, however.

Items Not Eligible for Return

Certain products, such as the used or modified forms of mining machines, software, and digital goods, are not entitled to returns and refunds. The company reserves the right not to accept returns on a product that has been damaged accidentally or is improperly used or for putting unauthorized repairs.

Exchanges and Replacements

If you receive a defective or incorrect product, you may be eligible for an exchange or replacement. Please contact our support team for assistance.

For any return requests or otherwise, you can always contact us at info@upacrav.com, and we would be glad to help!
