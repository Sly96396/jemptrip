Who We Are

Made with Simple Intentions

Origin

Began small with a love for baking

Jemptrip began this journey with a small kitchen and the idea that delicious cakes could easily bring people together. Each bite of our cakes communicates this approach.

Approach

Each recipe is created with honest work

No inefficiency and no shortcuts. Only honest methods, quality ingredients, and the goal of keeping it lovable and satisfying. Our approach undoubtedly states who we are.

People

Designed around trust and satisfactory service

From the moment you surf our cakes on our platform to cutting the last piece, we have put value in ease, hospitality, and clear communication with each customer. Our number one motto is to make you happy.

Vision

Focused on making daily moments sweeter

Not all great cakes need a big celebration. Our experienced cakemakers make cakes for calm evenings, small joys, and fun gatherings.

We are your reliable online destination for ordering delicious and fresh cakes whenever you need them. Enjoy new, fresh flavors like never before.
