Privacy Policy

  • Home
  • Privacy Policy

At Jemptrip, we think that you have the right to transparency with how your details are processed. No hidden doors, no jargon—just an open and clear description of what we are collecting, why we collect it, and how you are in control.

This policy is in effect when you are on our website, browsing through our cakes, placing your order, or just browsing around. We are USA-based, and we abide by the Privacy Act of 1974, which dictates how we are to handle personal data prudently.

I. What We Keep (And Why)

We gather just the minimum amount of information necessary in order to send your orders your way smoothly and securely. Your contact number, delivery address, and email are included. If you have an account, your login credentials are securely stored. We don't gather any excess that we need in order for you to enjoy what you have come here for—freshly baked cakes with purpose.

We may also gather browsing behavior (such as your device type or the pages you visit) in order to learn how our website is accessed. This assists us in operating efficiently without any hitches.

II. How We Keep It Safe

We employ secure digital practices that guard your information in the background. No cutting corners, no loose ends. Access is limited, and all is handled with purpose and respect.

III. Sharing Isn't Our Way

We don’t share your details with anyone. We don’t sell, rent, or give them away to anyone else who isn’t directly related to delivering your order. If we do need someone else (a delivery partner, for instance), we make sure they handle it with the same attention.

IV. Your Voice, Always

Feel free to contact us anytime you'd like to see, edit, or have us delete your data. Simply send us an email at info@jemptrip.com, and we’ll set you up with what you can do. Your data should never be something you need to go hunting for or be stressed about.

V. A Few Last Thoughts

It is subject to change from time to time as circumstances change, but when that does happen, we will publish the revised version here. You won’t be kept in the dark. Thanks for taking a few minutes with us—not just for indulging in our cakes, but for seeing how we function behind the scenes.

We are your reliable online destination for ordering delicious and fresh cakes whenever you need them. Enjoy new, fresh flavors like never before.
