Privacy Policy

Introduction

We are dedicated to safeguarding your privacy and handling your personal data with respect. This Privacy Policy outlines how we obtain, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our site. You consent to the practices set forth in this document by visiting our website. If you do have any questions, you can reach out to us at info@upacrav.com.

Information We Collect

When you visit our site, order with us, or communicate with us, we can collect:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and delivery information when you place an order.
  • Technical Data: IP address, type of device, browser information, and website browsing patterns.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: We enhance your experience on our website and tailor our services to your needs.

How We Use Your Information

We employ the data gathered to:

  • Process and send your orders.
  • Answer questions and customer support queries.
  • Enhance our site and services.
  • Distribute promotional emails (if you choose to opt-in).
  • Fulfill legal and regulatory obligations.

Children's Website Usage

We do not intend for our website to be aimed toward children under the age of 13, nor do we willingly collect information from these children. Information that is found to have been submitted by children will be deleted immediately. Email at info@upacrav.com if a parent or guardian feels their child's data is stored with us.

PDPO Compliance (Personal Data Privacy Ordinance)

We follow PDPO as we are a Hong Kong-based company. As per the PDPO act of Hong Kong, we are entitled to:

  • Access and update your personal data.
  • Request erasure of data in specific circumstances.
  • Be informed about how your data is used.
